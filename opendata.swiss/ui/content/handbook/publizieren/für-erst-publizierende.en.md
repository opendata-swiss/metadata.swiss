## Overview of the required steps[](https://handbook.opendata.swiss/fr/content/publizieren/erstpublizierende.html#die-erforderlichen-schritte-im-uberblick)

In opendata.swiss, every [dataset](https://handbook.opendata.swiss/fr/content/glossar/begriffe.html#term-Dataset) and every user is linked to an organization. If you have never published data on opendata.swiss, please start by registering your organization and its users. Once registration is complete, use the [back-end](https://handbook.opendata.swiss/fr/content/glossar/begriffe.html#term-Backend) to complete the organization information and publish your dataset. User management is handled by the OGD secretariat.

* [Enter and manage users](https://handbook.opendata.swiss/fr/content/publizieren/erstpublizierende/kontakt-bfs.html)
* [Manage the profile](https://handbook.opendata.swiss/fr/content/publizieren/erstpublizierende/profil-einrichten.html)
