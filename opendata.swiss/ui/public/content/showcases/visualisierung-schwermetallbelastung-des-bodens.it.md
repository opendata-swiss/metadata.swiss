This visualisation of heavy metal soil contamination is based on the current data published by the Federal Office for the Environment on Lindas. The data is read online (SPARQL) and processed. The map display does not show the actual location of the sites, but rather the geographical centre of the municipality. The visualisation was created with visualize.admin.ch.
**Visualize.admin.ch** is a visualisation tool for Swiss Open Government Data. The tool enables users to visualise data in real time and to share these visualisations or embed them in websites. Visualize.admin.ch makes the data in the federal government's linked data platform easier to understand and access. **[https://visualize.admin.ch](https://visualize.admin.ch/)**
